# Premium-But-Fair Monetization Strategy

## Current Revenue Streams (Both Games)
1. Game Passes (one-time): 2x Income ($199), Auto-Buy ($149), VIP ($399), Speed ($99)
2. Dev Products (repeatable): Cash Packs ($49/$199), Instant Rebirth ($99), Temp Boost ($29)
3. Daily engagement: login rewards, quests, promo codes

## Enhanced Monetization (To Implement)

### Tier 1: Starter Pack (NEW — highest converting item on Roblox)
- **"Starter Bundle"** — R$99 one-time
- Includes: 5,000 cash + 2x boost for 10 min + exclusive "Founder" name tag
- Only available to players who haven't bought anything yet
- Displayed prominently after first rebirth: "Congrats! Grab the Starter Bundle to go 2x faster!"
- **Why it works:** Low price point, high perceived value, captures first purchase

### Tier 2: Value Packs (NEW — bundle discounts)
- **"Mega Bundle"** — R$499 one-time
- Includes: 2x Income pass + Auto-Buy pass (normally R$348 separate = 30% savings)
- **"Ultimate Bundle"** — R$799 one-time
- Includes: ALL 4 game passes (normally R$846 = 5% discount + exclusive badge)

### Tier 3: Limited-Time Offers (NEW — FOMO mechanics)
- After rebirth 3: "FLASH SALE: 2x Income pass for R$149 (25% off!) — 24 hours only"
- Weekend events: "Double XP Weekend — all income 2x for 48 hours" (free, drives engagement)
- Monthly new promo codes (shared on social media to drive traffic)

### Tier 4: Cross-Promotion (Portfolio)
- In Business Tycoon: "Check out our new game SPACE TYCOON! Use code BUSINESS for 10K free credits!"
- In Space Tycoon: "Love space? Try BUSINESS TYCOON! Use code SPACE for $10K free cash!"
- Both games share a Roblox Group — members get exclusive benefits

## Pricing Psychology
- All passes end in 9 (R$99, R$149, R$199, R$399)
- Starter Bundle is the "anchor" — cheap enough for impulse buy
- VIP is the "aspirational" — expensive but visible (VIP buildings flex on other players)
- Cash Packs are the "whale" stream — repeatable, scales with progression
